Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the IB Partnership Program

What is an Introducing Broker (IB)?

An Introducing Broker is a partner who refers traders to our platform and earns commissions on their trading activity. You don't need to handle client funds or provide trading services directly.

How much can I earn as an IB Partner?

Earnings depend on your referral volume. You can earn up to 40% revenue share on trading volumes from your referrals. The more active traders you bring, the higher your earnings.

When do I get paid?

We offer daily payouts with no minimum withdrawal limits. Your commissions are calculated and paid directly to your account every day.

Do I need a license to become an IB?

In most cases, you don't need a specific license to become an IB Partner. However, you must comply with local regulations and our partnership agreement.

Can I have sub-IBs under me?

Yes! Our sub-IB program allows you to build a network of partners under you and earn commissions from their referrals as well.
