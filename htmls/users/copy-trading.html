Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about copy trading

What is copy trading?

Copy trading allows you to automatically mirror the trades of a professional trader in your own account. Every trade they make is replicated in your account proportionally.

Do I need trading experience?

No experience is needed! Copy trading is designed for beginners and experienced traders alike. You can start copying with just a few clicks.

How much money do I need to start?

The minimum investment for copy trading is $500. You can start small and scale up as you become more comfortable with the system.

Can I stop copying at any time?

Yes! You have full control over your account. You can pause or stop copying trades at any time with no penalties.

How are trades copied to my account?

Trades are copied automatically through our secure platform. When the master trader opens or closes a position, the same trade is executed in your account proportionally.
