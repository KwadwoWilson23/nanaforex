Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mentorship programs

What level of experience do I need to join?

We have programs for all levels - from complete beginners to experienced traders. The Foundation Program is perfect if you're just starting out, while the Pro and Elite programs are designed for traders with some experience.

How are the mentoring sessions conducted?

All sessions are conducted live via video calls. You'll have access to weekly group sessions and one-on-one coaching depending on your program level. All sessions are recorded so you can review them anytime.

What makes the mentorship different from the Academy?

The Academy is self-paced learning with pre-recorded content. The Mentorship is live, interactive, and personalized. You get direct access to mentors, real-time feedback, and customized guidance based on your trading style and goals.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all mentorship programs. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund your investment in full.

Can I upgrade my program later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher program at any time. You'll only pay the difference in price, and your remaining mentorship time will be adjusted accordingly.
