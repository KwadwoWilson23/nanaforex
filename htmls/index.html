Frequently Asked

Answers to common questions

Do I need prior trading experience?

No. The mentorship starts from the absolute basics — what forex is, how the market moves, how to read charts — and builds up to professional strategy over 12 weeks.

How much money do I need to start?

You can join the free Telegram with $0 to see how we work. Copy trading starts at $500 minimum. The Funded Trader Program lets you trade firm capital up to $200,000 without personal risk.

Is my capital safe with copy trading?

You keep full control of your account through HFM. You can pause, unlink, or withdraw at any time. Nana Forex never touches your funds — we only trigger the trade copies.

How do the funded accounts work?

You pay a challenge fee (from GHS 1,000), pass a two-phase evaluation of consistent, risk-managed trading, and get funded with up to $200,000 of firm capital. You keep 80–90% of profits.

How do I contact Emmanuel or the team?

The fastest ways are the Telegram community and WhatsApp (+233 247 107 781). You can also use the contact form on the Contact page — we reply within 24 hours.

Where is Nana Forex based?

We're headquartered in Accra, Ghana, and serve traders across West Africa and beyond. Mentorship is delivered live online, so you can join from anywhere.
